    * <span data-ttu-id="2e424-128">Estados de energía y red</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2e424-128">Network and power states</span></span>
    * <span data-ttu-id="2e424-129">Condiciones en uso</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2e424-129">In-use conditions</span></span>
    * <span data-ttu-id="2e424-130">Ubicación geográfica del dispositivo</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2e424-130">Device geolocation</span></span>
